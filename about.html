Our Philosophy

We believe that anyone can be a great writer, if they write in the right enviornment. Kenneth A. Bruffee once said that writing is just thought or conversation displaced, and that to get better at it, you need to get better at conversing and thinking too. Here at the Writer's Hub, we hope to create a place where anyone can write, converse, and think with their peers. By breaking down barriers and bringing great minds together, we hope to unleash the writer in everyone.

"The most powerful words in English are 'Tell me a story,' words that are intimately related to the complexity of history, the origins of language, the continuity of the species, the taproot of our humanity, our singularity, and art itself"
-Pat Conroy

Our vision is for you to write as much as you can, and to interact with the community. We sincerely hope that you will find what you need to begin expressing yourself at the highest level possible.
